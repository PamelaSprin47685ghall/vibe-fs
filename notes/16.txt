ReviewSession Registry + Query:
Registry = Map<string, ReviewSession>
RegistryAction = Activate | Deactivate | Evict | Lock | Unlock | Accept | Reject | AddChild | Clear
reduce: 纯函数 Registry + Action → new Registry。
actionFor: ReviewResult → RegistryAction。
sessionIsActive/taskOf/stateOf/canTransition/versionOf: 查询函数。
reduceIfVersionMatches: 乐观锁 — 版本匹配才执行 reduce。
Effects 侧表：
SessionEffects = { pendingResolutions; abortSuppressors }
setPending/resolvePending/disposeSessionTree: 审查完成回调管理。
resolvePending: 触发 resolve + abort suppressor 清理，返回是否找到。
