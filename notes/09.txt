ToolPermission 权限矩阵：
ToolSemantic = AgentReport | BlockedShellTaskGrep | StealthBrowser | ReturnRoleEcho | TodoFamily | Read | WritePatchFamily | FuzzyGrep | SubagentWebSkillOrSubmit | Other
classifyTool: 工具名 → 语义分类，先 normalizeToolName。
canUseSemantic: (agent, semantic, tool) → bool，规则表：
  - AgentReport: 全允许
  - BlockedShellTaskGrep: 全禁止
  - StealthBrowser: 仅 browser agent
  - ReturnRoleEcho: 仅 return_{agent}
  - bookkeeper/meditator/executor: 全禁止
  - TodoFamily/Read: 全允许
  - reviewer/browser: 全禁止
  - investigator: 仅 SubagentWebSkillOrSubmit 中的 executor 允许
  - WritePatchFamily: investigator/manager 禁止
deniedToolsForHost: 给定 host+agent，过滤不可用工具。
